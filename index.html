CAPIZDENTA is a trusted dental clinic in Roxas City, Capiz, offering high-quality and accessible dental care at reasonable rates for the Capiznon community and beyond.

Our clinic is designed with patient comfort in mind — a calm, clean, and faith-filled environment where you don't feel like just another patient. The ambiance is soothing and relaxing, especially important for those who feel nervous about dental visits.

What We Offer

Our Dental Services

Comprehensive care from routine checkups to full smile transformations.

🩺

General Dentistry

Routine checkups, cleanings, fluoride treatments, and preventive care to keep your smile healthy long-term.

✨

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and aesthetic enhancements that help your smile radiate confidence and beauty.

🦷

Orthodontics

Braces and alignment solutions to correct bite and positioning issues. Appointments recommended.

🔧

Restorative Dentistry

Fillings, crowns, bridges, and dentures designed to restore full function and natural-looking aesthetics.

🚨

Emergency Dental Care

Prompt care for toothaches, broken teeth, dental trauma, and other urgent dental needs.

📷

Dental Radiology

Digital X-rays and radiological imaging to accurately diagnose conditions not visible to the naked eye.

🧼

Dental Hygiene

Oral hygiene coaching, professional scaling, and polishing to maintain a clean and healthy mouth.

👶

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, child-friendly dental care in a welcoming environment that makes young patients feel safe.
