Our Story

Thoughtful Dental Care
Rooted in Comfort

CAPIZDENTA brings professional dental care, gentle service, and a calm clinic experience to patients across Roxas City and the Capiznon community.

Who We Are

Where every smile is treated with purpose.

CAPIZDENTA is a dental clinic in Roxas City, Capiz, Philippines, dedicated to providing high-quality, accessible dental care to the Capiznon community.

We offer personalized general, cosmetic, orthodontic, pediatric, and restorative dentistry with a commitment to professional excellence and heartfelt service.

Our clinic is built on the belief that beauty and smiles are created, not just maintained. Every patient deserves to feel heard, cared for, and confident when they leave.

Clinic Philosophy

Prevention is better than cure.

We encourage every patient to visit before pain begins, because early care saves time, worry, and expense.

Our Mission

Care that protects every smile.

To enhance, restore, and care for the smiles of our community with expert hands and a compassionate heart, because a healthy smile today saves time, worry, and expense tomorrow.

Our Vision

A trusted dental home for Capiz.

To become a leading dental clinic known for excellence, accessibility, and patient-centered care, where every individual feels confident in their smile.
