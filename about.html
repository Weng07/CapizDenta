About
CAPIZDENTA

Who We Are

CAPIZDENTA is a dental clinic in Roxas City, Capiz, Philippines, dedicated to providing high-quality, accessible dental care to the Capiznon community. We offer personalized general, cosmetic, orthodontic, and restorative dentistry services with a commitment to professional excellence and heartfelt service.

Our clinic is built on the belief that beauty and smiles are created — not just maintained. Every patient who walks through our doors deserves to feel heard, cared for, and confident when they leave.

Our Mission

To enhance, restore, and care for the smiles of our community with expert hands and a compassionate heart — because we know that a healthy smile today saves time, worry, and expense tomorrow.

Our Values

Excellence — We maintain the highest standards of dental care and continuously improve our skills and practices.

Compassion — Every patient is treated as an individual, not a case number. We listen, we empathize, and we serve with purpose.

Accessibility — Quality dental care should be available to everyone. We offer reasonable rates without compromising on quality.

Faith — Our clinic is a faith-filled space, rooted in the belief that we are called to serve our community with integrity and love.

Visit Us

We are located in Roxas City, Capiz, Philippines. Our clinic is open Monday and Wednesday through Sunday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Tuesdays.

Walk-ins are always welcome for general consultations. Appointments are recommended for orthodontic and restorative procedures.
